TITLE: Statistics Act 1979

ACTNUMBER: 1979 No 12

STATUS: In Force

VERSION: Latest Version

AGENCY: National Statistics Office

DATEOFASSENT: 20 March 1979

COMMENCEMENT: see section 2

PARTNUMBER: 1

PARTTITLE: Preliminary Provisions

An Act to establish the National Statistics Office; to provide for the collection, compilation, analysis, and dissemination of official statistics; to coordinate statistical activities across Government; to prescribe statistical standards and ensure the integrity, independence, and confidentiality of statistical information; to provide for the conduct of surveys and censuses and the use of administrative data; to establish mechanisms for oversight, accountability, and performance review; to repeal the Statistical Collections Act 1952; and for related purposes.


SECTION: 1


HEADING: Short Title

1. This Act may be cited as the **Statistics Act 1979**.
2. In all other enactments, references to the Statistics Act shall be construed as references to this Act unless the context otherwise requires.
3. The short title may be abbreviated as “Stats Act 1979” in administrative instruments, registers, and citations.



SECTION: 2


HEADING: Commencement
        
1. This Act shall come into force on a date appointed by Order in Council made by the Governor-General on the advice of the Minister of Defence.
2. Different dates may be appointed for different Parts or provisions of this Act.
