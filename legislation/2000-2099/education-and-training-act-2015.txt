TITLE: Education and Training Act 2015

ACTNUMBER: 2015 No 53

TYPE: Act

STATUS: In force

VERSION: Latest

AGENCY: Ministry of Education

DATEOFASSENT: 1 January 2016

COMMENCEMENT: see section 2

PARTNUMBER: 1

PARTTITLE: Preliminary Provisions

LONGTITLE: An Act to reform and consolidate the laws relating to education and training in Square Country; to amalgamate existing education agencies into the Ministry of Education and the Square Qualifications Authority; to provide for a coherent, efficient, and accountable education system; to regulate early childhood services, schools, tertiary institutions, and vocational training; to establish a unified qualifications framework; and for related purposes.

HEADING: Short Title

This Act is the **Education and Training Act 2015**.
