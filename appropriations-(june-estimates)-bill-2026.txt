TITLE: Appropriations (June Estimates) Bill 2026

ACTNUMBER: 2026 No 04

TYPE: Bill

STATUS: Current

VERSION: Latest

AGENCY: The Treasury

DATEOFASSENT: see section 2

COMMENCEMENT: see section 2

PARTNUMBER: 1

PARTTITLE: Government Bill

SECTION: 1

HEADING: Title

These regulations are the Social Security (Minimum Income Guarantee and Business Hire-Drive Rebate) Regulations 2026.

SECTION: 2

HEADING: Commencement

This Act comes into effect the day it receives royal assent.

SECTION: 3

HEADING: Application

This Act applies to the financial term ending with 30 June 2026.

SECTION: 4

HEADING: Interpretation

In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,—
**"May financial term"** means the financial term ending with 30 June 2026.
**"Estimates"** means **The Estimates of Appropriations for the Government of Square Country for the Year Ending 30 June 2027.

SECTION: 5

HEADING: Appropriation

In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires—
**“digital platform”** means any internet-based service, application, or website that enables users to create, share, or view content, including social media platforms, video-sharing services, and messaging applications.
**“platform operator”** means any person or entity that owns, controls, or administers a digital platform on which social media advertising may be published.
**“social media advertising”** means any form of electoral advertising published, displayed, promoted, or disseminated on a digital platform, whether paid or unpaid, and includes sponsored posts, targeted advertisements, boosted content, and algorithmically distributed material.

SECTION: 6

HEADING: Appropriations

That all allocations within this document be accepted.
